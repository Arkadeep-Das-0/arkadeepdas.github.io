Research

Direction & experience

Where I want to focus, and the research work that has shaped how I get there.

My research interest is cybersecurity — specifically software and network security, cloud security, and the security of systems built on large language models. I'm drawn to the same question across all three: when we depend on a system, where does that trust actually live, and what happens when an adversary reaches it?

I come at this from an unusual angle. My published work is in applied machine learning, and I've spent two years building and operating real software and network infrastructure. That means I can read a model, ship a system, and reason about a network — and I want to turn that breadth into focused, rigorous security research.
